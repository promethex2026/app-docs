## What is PrometheX?

PrometheX is a **Prediction Market as a Service (PMaaS)** platform built on Arbitrum. Users trade on the outcomes of real-world events — politics, sports, crypto, culture — anything with a verifiable result.

The platform provides:

- **On-chain market making** via APMM (Automated Prediction Market Maker)
- **Gasless trading** through ERC-4337 account abstraction
- **Modular resolution** with UMA Optimistic Oracle and pluggable modules
- **White-label capabilities** for partners who want their own prediction market

## How It Works (30 seconds)

1. **Connect** — Log in with email or wallet via Privy. A smart account is created for you.
2. **Discover** — Browse trending markets or search by topic.
3. **Trade** — Pick an outcome, set your amount, confirm. No gas needed.
4. **Win** — When the market resolves, claim your winnings instantly.

## Architecture at a Glance

```
Frontend (React + Cloudflare Workers)
    ↓ HTTPS + SSE
Market Backend (Go, port 8000)
    ↓ gRPC
Market Service (Go, port 9000)
    ↓
PostgreSQL + Redis + Arbitrum
```
