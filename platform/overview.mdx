## Prediction Market as a Service

PrometheX gives you the complete infrastructure to launch prediction markets under your own brand. Smart contracts, liquidity engine, resolution oracle, APIs, admin tools, and a white-label frontend — all production-ready on Arbitrum.

You focus on your users. We handle the trading engine.

## How Integration Works

Choose the model that fits your product and engineering capacity.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick your integration model">
    **White-Label** for a turnkey branded experience, **Embedded** for drop-in widgets, or **API-Only** for full custom builds. [Compare models →](/platform/integration/integration-overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your tenant">
    We provision a tenant with your branding, domain, fee structure, and feature flags through the Admin Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy markets">
    Create markets via the Admin API or dashboard. Each market deploys an isolated smart contract via the PredictionFactory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Your users start trading. Gas is sponsored via ERC-4337 account abstraction — they never need to hold ETH.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Ships Out of the Box

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Market lifecycle** | Create, fund, trade, settle, claim — fully managed on-chain |
| **N-outcome markets** | Binary (Yes/No) or multi-option markets with configurable weights |
| **Gasless trading** | ERC-4337 account abstraction with Paymaster — zero gas for end users |
| **Social login** | Email, Google, Twitter, Discord auth via Privy — no wallet setup required |
| **Real-time data** | SSE streams for live prices, trades, and market state changes |
| **Community features** | Posts, comments, likes, reputation, leaderboards |
| **Gamification** | Invite system, task rewards, achievement badges |
| **Admin panel** | Multi-tenant RBAC dashboard for market ops and content management |
| **Analytics** | Volume, P&L, user metrics, and market performance tracking |
