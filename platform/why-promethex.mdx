</Tip>

## Key Differentiators

### APMM — Liquidity Without Market Makers

Most prediction markets rely on a Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) and active market makers to provide liquidity. No market makers means no liquidity, means no trades.

PrometheX's **Automated Prediction Market Maker** provides guaranteed liquidity from block one. Every market launches with a functioning order book — no need to source, onboard, or pay professional market makers.

### Modular Resolution

Resolution is the hardest part of prediction markets. PrometheX makes it pluggable.

| Resolution Module | How It Works | Best For |
|-------------------|-------------|----------|
| **UMA Optimistic Oracle** | Assert outcome → challenge window → DVM dispute escalation | Decentralized, trustless resolution for public events |
| **Multisig Resolution** | Designated signers vote on outcome | Editorial control, private markets, DAO governance |
| **Custom Module** | Implement the `IResolutionModule` interface | API-driven resolution, proprietary data feeds, game results |

<Info>
Resolution modules are hot-swappable at the factory level. You can run UMA Oracle for public markets and multisig for internal governance markets within the same tenant.
</Info>

### White-Label & Multi-Tenant

PrometheX isn't a platform you send users to. It's infrastructure that runs under your brand.

- **Custom domain** — `markets.yoursite.com`, not `promethex.market`
- **Full theming** — Colors, logos, typography match your brand
- **Feature flags** — Enable/disable trading, community, gamification per tenant
- **Tenant isolation** — Separate data, users, markets, and configurations per partner
- **Admin RBAC** — Role-based access control for your operations team
