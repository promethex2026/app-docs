## What Is USDC?

**USDC** (USD Coin) is a **stablecoin** — a digital token that's always worth \$1.00. It's issued by Circle and backed by real US dollars in reserve. On PrometheX, USDC is what you use to buy and sell shares.

Think of it as digital cash that lives on the blockchain.

## Mainnet: Getting Real USDC

<Info>
  PrometheX mainnet on **Arbitrum One** is coming soon. The instructions below are for when real trading goes live.
</Info>

When PrometheX launches on mainnet, you'll need real USDC on the **Arbitrum** network (a fast, low-cost blockchain built on top of Ethereum). Here are the most common ways to get it:

### Option 1: Buy USDC on a Centralized Exchange

The simplest route if you're starting from scratch:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy USDC on an exchange">
    Use an exchange like **Coinbase**, **Binance**, or **Kraken** to buy USDC with your bank account or credit card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw to Arbitrum">
    When you withdraw, select **Arbitrum One** as the network. Paste your PrometheX smart account address as the destination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done!">
    The USDC arrives in your smart account within a few minutes. You're ready to trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Always double-check that you're withdrawing to the **Arbitrum One** network, not Ethereum mainnet. Sending to the wrong network can delay your funds.
</Warning>

### Option 2: Bridge from Ethereum

If you already have USDC on Ethereum, you can move it to Arbitrum:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Arbitrum Bridge">
    Visit [bridge.arbitrum.io](https://bridge.arbitrum.io) and connect your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bridge your USDC">
    Select USDC, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction. The bridge moves your tokens from Ethereum to Arbitrum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer to your PrometheX account">
    If you bridged to an external wallet, send the USDC from there to your PrometheX smart account address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Bridging from Ethereum to Arbitrum typically takes about 10 minutes. You'll need a small amount of ETH on Ethereum to pay for the bridge transaction gas fee.
</Note>

### Option 3: Buy Directly on Arbitrum

Some services let you buy USDC directly on the Arbitrum network:

- **Ramp Network** — Buy with credit card or bank transfer
- **MoonPay** — Buy with card, available in many countries
- **Transak** — Another on-ramp option with multiple payment methods

These services deposit USDC straight to your wallet address on Arbitrum.

## Finding Your Wallet Address

To receive USDC, you'll need your smart account address:

1. Sign in to [PrometheX](https://staging.promethex.market)
2. Click on your **profile** or **wallet** icon
3. Copy your wallet address (it looks like `0x1234...abcd`)

This is the address you give to exchanges, bridges, or friends when they send you USDC.
