## What You Need

PrometheX staging runs on **Arbitrum Sepolia** testnet. To start trading, you need two tokens in your MetaMask wallet:

| Token | What It's For | How to Get |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| **Sepolia ETH** | Gas fees for claiming tUSDC and depositing | Free from external faucets |
| **tUSDC** | The trading token on PrometheX | Free from the tUSDC contract |

<Note>
All tokens on testnet have **no real value** — they're free practice money. You can claim as many times as you need.
</Note>

---

## Prerequisites: Set Up MetaMask

Before claiming tokens, make sure MetaMask is connected to the right network.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install MetaMask">
    If you don't have it yet, install MetaMask from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io/). Available as a browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, Brave, and Edge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Arbitrum Sepolia network">
    Open MetaMask and add the testnet. You can either:
    - Visit [chainlist.org](https://chainlist.org/?testnets=true&search=arbitrum+sepolia) and click **Add to MetaMask**
    - Or add manually with these settings:

    | Property | Value |
    |----------|-------|
    | Network Name | Arbitrum Sepolia |
    | RPC URL | `https://sepolia-rollup.arbitrum.io/rpc` |
    | Chain ID | `421614` |
    | Currency Symbol | ETH |
    | Block Explorer | `https://sepolia.arbiscan.io` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect MetaMask to PrometheX">
    Go to [staging.promethex.market](https://staging.promethex.market), click **Sign In** → **Connect Wallet** → select **MetaMask**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 1: Claim Testnet ETH

You need a small amount of Arbitrum Sepolia ETH to pay gas when calling the tUSDC faucet contract.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a faucet">
    Open any of these free faucets:

    | Faucet | Link | Notes |
    |--------|------|-------|
    | Alchemy | [faucets.alchemy.com](https://faucets.alchemy.com/faucets/arbitrum-sepolia) | Requires free Alchemy account |
    | QuickNode | [faucet.quicknode.com](https://faucet.quicknode.com/arbitrum/sepolia) | No account needed |
    | Chainlink | [faucets.chain.link](https://faucets.chain.link/arbitrum-sepolia) | Connect wallet to claim |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your MetaMask address">
    Copy your wallet address from MetaMask (click your address at the top to copy), then paste it into the faucet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim">
    Click **Send** or **Request**. You'll receive **0.01–0.1 ETH** within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Check your MetaMask balance — make sure you're on the **Arbitrum Sepolia** network. You should see your ETH balance updated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Most faucets have a 24-hour cooldown. If one is rate-limited or empty, try a different one from the list.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Claim tUSDC

tUSDC is the trading token on PrometheX staging. The contract has a public **faucet function** — anyone can mint test tokens directly through Arbiscan.

**tUSDC Contract:** [`0x934E616cA9538397E3f9a12895C40ea2E8C45E48`](https://sepolia.arbiscan.io/address/0x934E616cA9538397E3f9a12895C40ea2E8C45E48)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the contract on Arbiscan">
    Go to the [tUSDC Write Contract page](https://sepolia.arbiscan.io/address/0x934E616cA9538397E3f9a12895C40ea2E8C45E48#writeContract).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your MetaMask">
    Click **Connect to Web3** on the Arbiscan page and authorize your MetaMask. Make sure MetaMask is on the **Arbitrum Sepolia** network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Call the faucet function">
    Find the **`faucet`** function in the list and enter the amount you want. tUSDC uses **6 decimals**, so:

    | Amount | Value to Enter |
    |--------|----------------|
    | 100 tUSDC | `100000000` |
    | 1,000 tUSDC | `1000000000` |
    | 10,000 tUSDC | `10000000000` |

    Enter the value, click **Write**, and confirm the transaction in MetaMask.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tUSDC to MetaMask">
    To see your tUSDC balance in MetaMask:
    1. Click **Import Tokens** at the bottom of MetaMask
    2. Paste address: `0x934E616cA9538397E3f9a12895C40ea2E8C45E48`
    3. Symbol: `USDC`, Decimals: `6`
    4. Click **Add**

    Your tUSDC balance should now be visible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can call the faucet as many times as you want — there's no limit. Claim more whenever you need it.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Deposit tUSDC into PrometheX

Now that you have tUSDC in MetaMask, deposit it into PrometheX to start trading.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your wallet on PrometheX">
    Go to [staging.promethex.market](https://staging.promethex.market). Make sure you're signed in with your MetaMask wallet. Click your **profile icon** → **Wallet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Deposit">
    Click the **Deposit** button and enter the amount of tUSDC you want to deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and confirm">
    MetaMask will prompt you twice:
    1. **Approve** — Allows the PrometheX contract to spend your tUSDC
    2. **Deposit** — Transfers tUSDC from your wallet into the platform

    Confirm both transactions in MetaMask.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    Once confirmed, your balance updates on PrometheX. Head to the [Markets](https://staging.promethex.market) page to place your first trade!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
After depositing, all trades on PrometheX are **gasless** — the platform sponsors gas fees via account abstraction. You only need ETH for the initial faucet claim and deposit steps.
</Info>

## Mainnet: Getting Real USDC

<Info>
PrometheX mainnet on **Arbitrum One** is coming soon. The instructions below are for when real trading goes live.
</Info>

When PrometheX launches on mainnet, you'll need real USDC on the **Arbitrum** network. Common ways to get it:

### Option 1: Buy on an Exchange

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy USDC">
    Use an exchange like **Coinbase**, **Binance**, or **Kraken** to buy USDC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw to Arbitrum">
    When withdrawing, select **Arbitrum One** as the network. Paste your wallet address as the destination.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Always double-check that you're withdrawing to **Arbitrum One**, not Ethereum mainnet.
</Warning>

### Option 2: Bridge from Ethereum

If you already have USDC on Ethereum, bridge it via [bridge.arbitrum.io](https://bridge.arbitrum.io). Takes about 10 minutes.

### Option 3: Buy Directly on Arbitrum

Services like **Ramp Network**, **MoonPay**, and **Transak** let you buy USDC directly on Arbitrum with a credit card.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see my tUSDC in MetaMask">
    You need to import the token manually. In MetaMask: **Import Tokens** → paste `0x934E616cA9538397E3f9a12895C40ea2E8C45E48` → Symbol: `USDC` → Decimals: `6`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Faucet transaction fails on Arbiscan">
    You need Sepolia ETH for gas. If your ETH balance is zero, claim from one of the ETH faucets in Step 1 first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="MetaMask shows wrong network">
    Click the network dropdown in MetaMask and switch to **Arbitrum Sepolia** (Chain ID: 421614). If it's not listed, add it manually using the network details above.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deposit approval stuck or fails">
    If the approve transaction is pending too long, try **Speed Up** in MetaMask (click the pending tx). If it fails, make sure you have enough ETH for gas.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
