## Sign Up in 30 Seconds

PrometheX uses **Privy** (a sign-in service built for crypto apps) to let you create an account the same way you sign into any website — with your email, Google account, or Twitter. No browser extensions, no seed phrases, no crypto knowledge required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to PrometheX">
    Open [app.promethex.market](https://app.promethex.market) in your browser (desktop or mobile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign In">
    Tap the **Sign In** button in the top-right corner.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/user-guide/sign-in-dialog.png" alt="PrometheX sign-in dialog with email, social login, and wallet options" style={{borderRadius:'12px',maxWidth:'600px'}} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your method">
    Pick one of the following:
    - **Email** — Enter your email address and click the verification link sent to your inbox.
    - **Google** — Sign in with your Google account in one click.
    - **Twitter** — Authorize with your Twitter/X account.
    - **Crypto wallet** — If you already have a wallet like MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet, you can connect it directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in!">
    That's it. PrometheX automatically creates a **smart account** for you — a secure on-chain wallet powered by a **smart contract** (a program that runs on the blockchain). You don't need to manage keys or remember anything.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your smart account is created the first time you sign in. It's linked to your login method (email, Google, etc.), so you can always access it by signing in the same way.
</Tip>

## What's a Smart Account?

A smart account is a next-generation crypto wallet. Here's how it compares to traditional wallets:

| Feature | Traditional Wallet | PrometheX Smart Account |
|---------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Setup | Install extension, write down seed phrase | Sign in with email/Google/Twitter |
| Gas fees | You pay for every transaction | PrometheX pays for you |
| Recovery | Lose your seed phrase = lose everything | Sign back in with your email/social account |
| Security | Controlled by a private key | Controlled by a smart contract with multiple safeguards |

<Info>
**Under the hood:** Your smart account uses a technology called **ERC-4337 account abstraction**. This is a blockchain standard that lets smart contracts act as wallets. It's what makes gasless trading possible — the platform can sponsor your transaction fees automatically.
</Info>

## Already Have a Crypto Wallet?

If you're a crypto-native user with an existing wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.), you can connect it directly:

1. Click **Sign In** → **Connect Wallet**
2. Choose your wallet provider
3. Approve the connection

PrometheX will still create a smart account linked to your external wallet. All trades go through this smart account so you get the same gasless experience.

<Note>
Whether you sign in with email or connect a wallet, you get the same smart account features — zero gas fees, on-chain settlement, and full control of your funds.
</Note>

## What Happens to My Money?

Your funds live in your smart account on the **Arbitrum** blockchain (a fast, affordable network built on Ethereum). PrometheX **never holds your funds** — you always have full control. We can't move, freeze, or take your money.

This is called being **non-custodial**: you own your assets, not us.
