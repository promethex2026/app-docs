## Trade on What You Know

PrometheX is a **prediction market** — a place where you buy and sell shares based on whether you think something will happen. Think of it like a stock market, but instead of companies, you're trading on real-world events.

**Example:** "Will Bitcoin hit $100k by June 2026?"
- If you think **yes**, you buy **Yes shares**.
- If you think **no**, you buy **No shares**.
- If you're right, each share pays out **$1.00 USDC** (a digital dollar that's always worth $1).

That's it. No complex setup, no crypto wallet required, no gas fees to worry about.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use your email, Google, or Twitter account. PrometheX creates a **smart account** (a secure digital wallet managed by a smart contract) for you automatically — no seed phrases, no browser extensions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a market">
    Browse trending topics or search for something you're interested in. Each market shows a question and the current price of each outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buy shares">
    Choose an outcome, enter how much you want to spend, and confirm. That's one click — PrometheX covers all transaction fees for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get paid">
    When the event happens, the market resolves. If you picked the winning outcome, each of your shares is worth $1.00 USDC. Claim your winnings anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Prices

Every share on PrometheX is priced between **$0.00** and **$1.00 USDC**.

The price tells you what the crowd thinks the probability is:

| Share price | What it means |
|-------------|---------------|
| **$0.70** | The market thinks there's roughly a **70% chance** this outcome happens |
| **$0.30** | The market thinks there's roughly a **30% chance** |
| **$0.50** | It's a coin flip — the market is undecided |

<Tip>
Yes shares and No shares always add up to $1.00. If Yes is priced at $0.65, then No is priced at $0.35.
</Tip>

**Why does this matter?** If you think the real probability is higher than the current price, that share is a good deal. Buy low, sell high — or hold until the market resolves and collect $1.00 per winning share.

## Why PrometheX?

  <Card title="No Gas Fees — Ever" icon="gas-pump">
    Most crypto apps charge you a transaction fee ("gas") every time you do something. PrometheX pays all gas fees for you using a technology called **account abstraction** (a way for the platform to cover blockchain costs on your behalf).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign In Like Any App" icon="right-to-bracket">
    No crypto wallet needed. Sign in with your email, Google, or Twitter — just like any other website. A secure smart account is created for you behind the scenes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Everything On-Chain" icon="link">
    Your trades, positions, and payouts all happen on **Arbitrum** (a fast, low-cost blockchain built on top of Ethereum). Everything is transparent and verifiable — you don't have to trust us.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fair Resolution" icon="scale-balanced">
    Markets are resolved by a decentralized **oracle** (an independent system that verifies real-world outcomes). No single person decides who wins — the process is transparent and disputeable.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
